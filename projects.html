Computer Graphics Final Project - Spring 2019

Image Gallery (Click to expand)

×

OpenGL based game made for Computer Graphics class. You play as a destructive robot hell-bent on destroying downtown Manhattan, letting nothing get in your way. Boasts feature such as: Freely cruise through the city; turning at road intersections, Multiple toggle-able camera angles (9 total), Destroyable buildings with different durabilities (click to destroy), Random building generation for a different 20x20 block city every time, Head Rotation, Game Pausing, Easy to add new buildings in the future.
